About Fakir Ventures

A future-focused venture builder creating AI-native companies across high-impact global sectors with a dedicated team of 5 core members.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Fakir Ventures started with a vision to bridge the gap between artificial intelligence and traditional industries. What began as a small initiative with 5 passionate individuals has now grown into a venture powerhouse with 50+ portfolio companies across 10 sectors.

Our name "Fakir" reflects our humble beginnings and our commitment to staying grounded while pursuing ambitious goals. We believe in the power of intention, as our guiding principle says: إنما الأعمال بالنيات (Actions are judged by intentions)

Our Guiding Principles

  • • Innovation with Integrity: Building technology that serves humanity
  • • Sustainable Growth: Creating lasting value, not quick profits
  • • Team First: Our 5 core members are our greatest asset
  • • Global Impact: Solving real problems across borders
🎯

Our Mission

To build scalable, technology-driven businesses that solve real-world challenges. By combining artificial intelligence, data infrastructure, and operational excellence, we create sustainable competitive advantages in emerging industries while staying true to our ethical foundations.

👁️

Our Vision

To become a global venture powerhouse shaping the next generation of AI-driven enterprises in agriculture, fintech, energy, healthcare, logistics, media, and beyond — while empowering 100+ startups by 2030.
